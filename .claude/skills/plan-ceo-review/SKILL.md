# Plan CEO Review

Use this skill for product-level review of scope, tradeoffs, and likely value.

## Repo Rules

- Keep the review strategy-focused and grounded in the current repo.
- Ignore vendored telemetry, `~/.gstack`, and global-home assumptions.
- Deliver a concrete recommendation with reasons.

## Workflow

1. Restate the goal and scope in product terms.
2. Identify expansion, hold, or reduction options.
3. Recommend the most defensible scope with explicit tradeoffs.
