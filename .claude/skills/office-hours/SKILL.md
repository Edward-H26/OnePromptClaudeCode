# Office Hours

Use this skill for early-stage product thinking, scoping, and challenge of assumptions.

## Repo Rules

- Keep this workflow plan-first and decision-focused.
- Ground the discussion in the current repo and the user's stated goal.
- Ignore vendored telemetry, proactive prompts, and global-home setup.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the goal, success criteria, and constraints.
2. Challenge ambiguous or weak assumptions.
3. Produce a decision-ready plan or recommendation before implementation.
